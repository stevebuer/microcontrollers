# STM32F103C8T6

I have two of these board, one in the Blue Pill for factor and the other as a dev/breakout board shown below.

<img src="STM32FxCxT6-devboard.jpg" width="200">

## Device Specs

* Arm Cortex-M3 @ 72 Mhz
* 64K Flash
* 20K SRAM
* Temp. Sensor
* USB 2.0, CAN, i2C, SPI, 3 UART
* SWD/JTAG
